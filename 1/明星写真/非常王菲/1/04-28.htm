Faye reveals herself


November 1998 City Magazine 266 ( Hao Wai ) Private Eye Far distance close contact , Faye reveals herself

The different kinds of feelings in Faye's current times

Far distance look Faye Text : Wendy

Nov 1998 issue, "City Magazine" have Faye. Its been about one billion, two thousand, six hundered and fourteen million and four thousand seconds apart, since Faye last appeared on "City Magazine" cover in 1994 Dec, its also about when she was doing her concert time then.

In all, I know two of her. One is the friendly, just like all the other Artists, Wong Jing Wen , with an english name called Shirley, another is Detach far away from the crowd, media , and has a strong character of her own, Faye Wong.

No matter which times Faye you or me like, cannot deny that the Faye now , her name is already a "fairy tale", one who breaks through the entertainment game rules, but who still can keep her super position.

I do not intend to discuss (analysis) faye's current times, no matter how the media describes her attitude, her family situation, her rumours and moods, those who like her only will love her even more of her doing her way kind of handling method. Her albums are still the pop charts Best-Seller, her every performance is defintely worth the price, her Position still remains as the Asian Pop Queen ..... I feel, this should be one criteria in the heart that reflects a normal person cannot or do not dare to do things, Faye has the capability to do it very cooly and nicely with no regrets. A truly worthwhile idol mainly needs this kind of not give in easily to challenges kind of brave attitude.

Artiste must definitely have Charisma, this is the first criteria on the list. Faye's Charisma has two types : includes appearance and feeling. Faye's appearance originally is very beautiful, with a pair of big eyes and sharp face, plus a heavenly good and clear voice. I read some articles before regarding Faye, when she first began as a model, but if she dun sing, then it will have been such a waste in her talent.

With Faye's height, her character as a singer, isn't it what a true version of a Model ?

I have always love to see faye Mtvs, because every time I can feel that the Creative Team's production efforts, from the director to the scenes to image to her performance, you will be able to feel that kind of effort that they have put in. Till to date, the mtvs I like most are "I'm Willing", "Chess" and " I dun wish to be this either".

Besides, liking the Cool Cool faye, also like her excellent choice of dressing, a very expensive shirt wore on her will not give the fake impression of the richness , this is really a heavenly gift that we cannot envy about.

I have seen many Faye's live performances, personally experience that kind of with the live audience and fans, that kind of natural and not fake good responses. Every moment and smile of faye will make the audience to scream loudly and led to great amount of applause and cheering .......

The most recent deep impression I had was at the end of September , under FM 104 " Good Friends Concert", together with Faye performing was her good friend, Naying and Jeff Chang. That night Faye was High High, heard it was due to she drank a little wine before that. I feel that that night , faye's performance was specially relax , specially happy, because she sang all her favourite songs, have her own songs, also have other people's songs, and songs she co-operate with her two good friends.

Actually this is not too hard to understand, one normal person find one job that she likes, will also be very enthusiastic about it. Thus, since she came back from America , she formally changed back to her Faye Wong status to face the audience, music style also change to all starts from herself, Faye sings with even more feelings and also gets herself involved in the compositions work and gets her local musicians and their works. No doubt most of her more popular songs have some commercial factors in them, but we cannot deny that she indeed have lead the Hong Kong music lovers to have more contact and wider exposure with more alternative music and another listening sky.

Some people say Faye is a happy woman. According to old sayings as a measurement, one who is married, have a child, this woman is a happy family life model. But Faye is not one who suits that kind of life, and live as a rich lady and people serving her, just this physic this round, she is even more fit now than before she gave birth to Tong Tong, especially is her two skiny shoulders and her 24 inches waist. I think many young mothers also wish to learn from her how she keep fit.

Heavenly born couple, Douwei and Faye, never shown their happiness and sweetness in front of people, but the kind of chemistry can be clearly shown in their music. I always dun envy those kind of loving couples, kissing and holding hands now and then, this kind of gauge is too shallow. That old song " Exit" , isn't it the best thing to varify their love ?

Its almost been four years. Faye is going to have her second solo concert. From her experience in this line and her popularity rate to calculate, she cannot be considered as one who makes a lot of money as a singer. If not, she can have a concert every year and her income will be large enough to buy many rich houses in Hong Kong already.

I'm beginning to wait for that one billion, two thousand, six hundered and fourteen million and four thousand seconds later , going to happen soon event ......1998 December Christmas belongs to Faye Wong. What will you expect in one Sing & Play Big World ?

Loyal Fan true words Text : Zhao An

I never say I have an idol, Faye is one of them.

Like a person, like a thing, can have no reason, just that feeling, like is like, dun like is dun like, no need any explanations. Some people just have that kind of attraction that will make to like them naturally, Faye is that kind of people.

I met Faye once. That was during one weekend, in evening, in Zhong Huan Galleria's Joyce saw Faye. She was shopping happily, no one dares to go up to disturb her. When leaving, I met her again at the Escalator, that time Jean Paul Gaultier was still there. It was there Faye left a signature for me, when she was leaving, she said Bye Bye, her attitude was very friendly.

Many friends around me like Faye too, say Faye dun need any words to describe, everything about her is all totally in her music, her music is the best bridge to communicate between her and her fans. Any time you pick any one of her Cds, listen carefully to every note, every word, plus that light, soft, clear voice, seems you can understand her a bit more. Faye's voice, even if is simply "la la ....oh oh ...." is already very attractive, is even touching and better than those songs with lyrics, no wonder people say woman have a soft, nice voice is a gift from Heaven.

You say Faye beautiful or not ? I say no. Some people have all the 5 parts of the face very very beautiful, but lack a kind of sophistication; Faye do not have a perfect 5 parts, but when all is put together brings out a kind of very easy feeling to people, especially is her pair of talking eyes. People says this type of eyes is best for acting, but Faye didn't choose this road, till to date her only work is "ChungKing Express." Besides, the story is attractive, is that Faye's Pure, very natural way of acting attracts people. Though there's some who criticize on her acting, but I like and thats it. Good things sometimes is just like that, comes naturally. If purposely go and do something, the results might not be as good as expected. Everything is the same, goes with the feelings!

Faye's life has long been exposed, by coincidence, her career is a singer, everyone can only see her in the screen. All those want to know stuff, have all been written on those newspaper reports and articles .....all have been reported, those kind of shopping and buying houses stuff , leave to them to write lah! I'm still more concerned about Faye's music.

Faye, still is one who have a good voice and a normal woman.

Faye Wong Q & A Text : Jay C

Around this time, Faye is very very busy, album promotions, concerts preparations , but she still help "City Magazine" to do the interview below :

City : Music, what is it to you ? Faye : Music, to me is part of my life, just like my daily life, is also part of life, like a learning bridge, is everything I learn.

City : Towards your singing career, do you have any ambitions or dreams ? Faye : I never treat singing as my career, never thought of handling it, singing is my interest, on the surface now, it seems like my job, but I try not to think in this way, still treat it as my interest. In this way, will be more simple and happy.

City : Be popular, albums sell well or not, is that important ? Faye : Cannot say is not important, but is not the most important. I feel do one thing that you like and receive others agreement and compliments is a very happy thing. I will do things that I have interest in, feel that I can do it, and can do it well things. I will take note of others liking of me,but this is not what I wanted most.

City : As for backstage work, you did your own compositions before, write lyrics, background vocals, next step what position will you try ? Will try to learn any instruments ? Faye : Actually I dun know what position or sort, now I do what I am able to do , and try to do things that interest me, can do it or not is another thing. I hope I can complete one full album by myself, but for now is still a dream. I hope it can come true, but don't know when. Learn instruments ? Very long ago, I did thought of that, but I do not wish to force myself because of learn so go and learn, let nature take its own course.

City : Under not liking to do promotions but still need to do, will you feel is a kind of give in ? Faye : Sometimes give in is necessary, no one can forever in one life do everything that he or she likes to do, life, in this kind of dilemma, give in is necessary.

City : Will you suddenly disappear from the entertainment scene, live a simple normal life ? Faye : I dun know, not possible to plan. But I feel disappear from the entertainment scene and living a simple normal life is two matters. Not much clashes, most important is in one's heart, even though you are in the entertainment scene, you can also lead a normal simple life.

City : Love to film in movies ? Faye : As far till now, cannot say I am very into it, for me, movies is more hard to capture, I have a higher sensitivity to music. But if meet a good script, good role, I will go and try, but afraid cannot make it.

City : Are you someone who plans, plan life, plan work ? Faye : Feels that this varies from person to person, I'm someone who changes quite often, dun like to plan ! I will rely on my feelings in life to do things. Planning is not very useful to me, not suitable too.

City : After having Tong Tong, have your views in life change ? Faye : I dun feel have much changes, before and after are the same.

City : Do you feel you are full of happiness ? Faye : I dun really understand the definition of happiness, feel is a very extract concept. I feel my current life is quite happy, best wish that I can be happy.

City : Normally when you need not work, what do you love to do most ? Faye : Love to play, as long is play, ha !

City : You love to dream? Faye : Everyone have dreams, I also, but not those kind of day dreams, not that kind of serious people.

City: At this moment, what do you wish to do? Faye : At this moment, most wish to finish this interview.
