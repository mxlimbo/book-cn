Faye's Sing & Play World Tour


Totally Exciting Faye's Sing & Play World Tour - Hong Kong Concert 
From i-weekly, by Liu Jie Qi (hong kong exclusive report)

Intro :
This time, Faye is very outstanding as compared to what I saw her at her last concert in Singapore a few years back. This time is more exciting! Not only because of her wide range of outfits and hairstyles that were added with more new ideas, and also not because she sang over 30 plus songs that are all good songs of different styles, its more not because of the large amount of investments that is spend on the stage design. Most importantly, what I saw was : Faye ....that has more open up herself, reveal out her own character and is not far away form us.

Report :
January 9, 8pm, I rushed to the Hong Kong Stadium to catch the last nite of 'Faye's Sing & Play World Tour Hong Kong Concert'. Heard that is the most cold nite since Christmas, but there were lots of people, all with excited looks and with hands and legs full with anticipation, filled the big stadium.

It's already the last day of the concert, but the one million and two thousand seats were all fully taken up. Other than the times, when the stars , like Kuang Ming Quan, Chen Yu Lian, Luo Jia Yin, Du Ke Feng and others caught the attention of the media; rest of the time, the whole stadium is waiting patiently to be with Faye for their last date.

Starting off with 3 songs of her own compositions, 'Love Life', ' Exasperation Times' & 'Bored'. After these 3 songs, Faye said to the audience " Tonite is the 17th nite of my concert since I sing from Christmas, and also is the last nite, You are happy or not happy ? happy ah ...? why happy ? ...haha.....I very happy wo ....because from tonite onwards, I can finally take a good rest."

The continuous 17 nites, besides having to stay in a good composure, not only sing, sing, sing, change, change, change ( 6 sets of outfits), she also needs to wear the 'restricted no shoes, world's first type' of high heel shoes to walk in the water, also walk on the high and elevated stage areas, and sometimes unexpectedly, suddenly appears off the stage. If really think about it, its really quite tiring, especially for Faye, who is afraid of heights and sometimes walk not too stable.

Compared to the past, which was more simple, this time is obviously of a very high standard. The circulating stage that goes round really helps to bring the distance closer between faye and her audience. I see a Faye, that is continously waving and shaking hands, face filled with smiles and happy to receive good compliments from the fans , like ' You are very beautiful.'

Seems like we can see, Faye has already removed 'the distance is beauty' kind of thinking.

In the concert, theres many things that allows Faye's cute and fun loving character to flow naturally, e.g : the goodluck hedgehog, cartoon animated scenes, the pool next to the stage, her daily life shots that is captured like a documentary, plus lots of live responses, move with the beat and rhythm of the music, and legs swaying along, all these reveals Faye's forwardness and childlike manners.

As per normal, Faye still talks quite little, in the whole 2&1/2 hrs concert, she only sings a sing after another, "chess, cold war, an yong, sky,face,lost road,dreamlover,sleepwalk, forgive myself,doomsday,degeneration,angel,nostalgia,dream awaken,brink of love and pain, love commandments, one person two roles, di-dar, exit, ai-mei, you happy I happy, dated, repayment, I'm willing, Last forever and No regrets."

Never tried before seeing a concert can be so enjoyable, after 30 over songs, Faye's standard never dropped at all, you really cannot be not amazed by her ! Compared to the past, only standing on the stage and swaying the long sleeves of hers, this time Faye's concerts is all areas have added in lots of new concepts and ideas, but all these selling points did not waver our attention.

We all saw....except Faye, still is Faye, is only Faye.
