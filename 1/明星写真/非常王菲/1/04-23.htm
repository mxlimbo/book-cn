Faye Wong *Amoeba #58 Nov '98 Faye File

 

Before I start to elaborate on the article below, the writer hope that I can temporaily change singer, Faye Wong into ( sort of a product), this means that from this moment onwards, in the article that refers to Faye, is no longer regarding her as a singer, no longer Dou Wei's wife, no longer the active and cute tong tong's mum, in here ' Faye Wong' , is a business product, is a brand. This is because the writer will look from the marketing point of view to find out about the secrets of faye success.

Eighties market belongs to ( Anita Mui)'s - mysterious background, hundreds of images, well-verse in songs, lots of rumours, be the Best Female Actress.......that time Anita is about this.

Late eighties to early nineties , the main leader is (Sandy Lam) - unique image, sophisticated charism, mature feeling, low profile, can sing fast and slow songs well, special manners ......that time Sandy is really like this.

From the middle nineties and after ------ no a particular direction, character of a star, black mouth black look, anyhow shout and jump, yi yi he he , married and have a daughter, asia heavenly queen ........this kind of 'faye' is still hot in the market.

Cannot deny that in the local hongkong entertainment scene, its always that its siding with the males and bias against females, however also cannot deny that local hongkong female singers are of a higher caliber than the males. Among the vast green, there's a spot of red , Anita Mui is even more outstanding than the guys, and can be on the same level as them; Sandy Lam firstly, due to her in-born character, always is forever doing her best all the while, forever seems like is only smiling and watching the storm; the only one singer that can rise up to the top and be adored by millions out there, is only Faye Wong ------ when Faye just give a simple smile to the reporter's camera, it will become the headlines in the news, heavenly king maybe is only under the goddess.

If Faye is a brand name , a product. First stage : Wong Jing Wen times --- Product Introduction

In the late eighties, Chen Xiao Bao, a musician with many years of experience in this field found Faye who is from China ,and came over to hongkong recently, relying on the good image and beautiful voice, signed a contract and became one of the singers under Xin Yi Bao. In order to let Faye to be easily recognized and be accepted by the hongkong people, Faye Wong is changed name to "Wong Jing Wen", and was also called herself Shirley, becoming one of our local new female singers. Her debut album was launched in year 1989.

At that time, Guan Shu Yi is already moving towards sing and dance kind of style and with a wild type of character ,sings the song 'Lovers under the stars lit sky'. Sandy Lam also change her style, her city rhythm wave rush through the whole city. Her record company did not choose for her any of those kind of pop songs and image that was popular at that time, luckily what she got wasn't too far away from that. " Wong Jing Wen" is definetly not a bad album and is a steady debut. Songs like " No choice that day', ' If there's fate', till today still remains as frequent guests in late night programs.

During this time, though the record company haven't really successfully made " Wong Jing Wen' be very readily accepted by the hongkong people, but least it lets quite a number of music lovers to be familar with this brand "Wong Jing Wen". At least in the area of product awareness, the record company and the singer have made it !

After winning the new comer's award, "Wong Jing Wen" can say makes it to a stepping stone and things gets better and better. Songs like " Everything", " Continue to wait", " Thanks to him", etc makes people realizes her high pitch, R & B are that powerful. The producer also started to discover her voice range ---- as for pitch high or low has some directions, however, as for the expressions to show the feelings for the songs still have some diffculties to catch it. Seniors in this line give their opinions naturally: currently all singers sing songs totally lack of feelings, sing songs is only like to practise between the high notes skies. Its time not only to work hard, we now know that time "Wong Jing Wen" sing works that are not Faye Wong's favourites, its no wonder that the level of involvement definitely will have a deduction then.

Clever Faye finally is not willing to linger in between only the idols and powerful sector, decided to break through herself, thus in 1992, she decided to go to America to upgrade herself for half a year. There's no big news on it . There's only one or two compliations to recap on her songs, but the response turned out to be very good.

Second Stage : Came back from America to Hong Kong, Change back to Real Name --- Product Relaunch

After returing to Hong Kong, the media naturally used a description like a "total makeover / change" to describe Faye Wong. It was 1992. Anita Mui at that time have not retired yet but her new ideas and concepts are getting less popular ; Sally Yeh always full of feelings and macho label, after blessings and goodbye is good luck and care; Sandy's market estimation was wrong,overestimated the hongkong fans, "Wild Flower" move too fast, fans start to change .....in the end, "Easily Hurt Woman" very easily raised Faye Wong up to the first-line singers, and became very very popular : "Easily Hurt Woman" this song was broadcast over the radio stations for over half a year, when "No Regrets" was released for barely 2 days, and not plug over the stations yet, it was already very familiar with everyone in the streets already! " Liu Fei Fei" also overturn the mainstream style, thus makes " Faye Wong" power even obvious, " Cold War" , this type of "Alternative" music is also saved by her...... under the media support and promotion, the efforts are all really helpful. (Business stations can be said have put in their great efforts.), this made at this time "Faye Wong" like electricity , successfully made up to the top places. No matter big or small shops in the areas also play Cranberries, Cocteau Twins, Tori Amos, Bjork , etc ..type of Faye songs, thus can really prove "Faye Wong" influences. (Until today, Cranberries " No Need to Argue" is still very popular in these areas.)

Image is always "Faye Wong" most successful area, that is also a very important reason why she can stand out among the others. Dunno know if its because she's been abroad that brings the confidence, after Faye Wong returned to HongKong, she seems like beginning to realize her own uniqueness. She matched a black netted top with a very short leather skirt for "Coming Home" as cover for a prelude. At a certain award ceremony, whole transparent top plus a shorts for a start, she formely became our local HongKong fashion sense of direction, also became foreign countries fashion stage, those received are mostly come from "Ying Lun Bing Tao" style. Luckily Faye Wong herself has her own qualifications, if not at that time she will be wearing a bad name.

Image is only one factor, music is still the main subject. After a few popular albums, Faye Wong met DouWei in the music .....from then Faye Wong found herself, "Wong Jing Wen" formely disappear from the music scene, Shirley only have a surname 'Guan', Surname with 'Wong' is called FAYE. After " Good to oneself" , besides the music is good to faye herself, it also move many of the music judges, business and art received both successful results. In local music scene, its very obvious --- best male and female singer forever is not the most popular male and female singer. The thing that can be said to really bring "Faye" into the whole of Asia market is the following few mandarin pieces : "Chess", "Sky" , "I'm Willing".

"Faye" finally at this time successfully positioned herself, theres lots of female singers, old, new all naturally have a tough competition, fake products suddenly all appeared in the market, all tried to learn "Faye".

Learn is learn. What Faye had done before, if another person do it again will definitely be said as "Learn Faye'. Result : learn Faye most likely will receive bad comments from the designers, learn Faye to get married have kid , might not definitely can receive a red carpet to welcome the whole family to come out together, learn Faye to be black face to try to be in the entertainment news, learn Faye singing, history has proven there's only a dead end.

But, faye also did learn from talents that only can she be better. Why all the other female singers are being commented till so badly, but she can be spared of this ? The main reason I believe is at that time "Faye" is the first comer. Besides became very fast to be the market leader. Looking at outside and inside, Faye might not be walking very fast, but other people can be walking a bit faster.

Third Stage : From Xin Yi Bao to EMI --- Product Sustain

Want to make the music lovers to continue support "Faye Wong", besides need to continue rely on good songs to treat the fans, appropriate variations to attract and maintain her own supporters also have a great effect.

In order to make her position different from the normal singers, "Faye Wong" in her strategy, forsake the usual methods that singers used to have a high apperance rate, promotions, talk nicely with the media, change to use her own way and style of handling things, with no fear attitude and daring style to fight with the media. Although at the beginning, maybe someoe (believe is someone from the record company) was worried about this, but this method of handling in the end resulted in a miracle effect! Not only the media is amazed, the general public also think that "Faye Wong" is really someone with her own character, successfully created the character building in the market.

This point for a product, is very very important, this is because if a product does not have a character that is recognised, when competitors appear, that product will be easily forgotten by its target customers, thus competiveness will be lessened. This can be used to explain why many female singers have failed.

After changing her record company, Faye announced that she will give up cantonese songs as her main hit songs, as she wants to be more involved in her own creations and forsake the past type of music style.

Present "Faye Wong" has really done that, no fake request, in the market, she can be seen as the most outstanding.

Fourth Stage : Present day, overshoot her direct competitors --- Direct Competition

For the past one or two years, "Faye Wong" in the local music market is still in the leading position, other brands even if sell similarly well, but the amount is still a distance from "Faye Wong", till "Zhang Hui Mei" appeared.

"Ah-Mei" appearance, to "Faye ", can be said is truly a competitor for the past few years, because in many areas, both are quite homogeneous products, both are using mandarin songs are their main hits. Singing styles can be said both have their own outstanding points. Normally in cases like people tend to like 'new things', fans are lost, such things is unavoidable. Even though so, want to replace "Faye Wong" market leader position, "Ah-Mei" seems to still have a distance to walk. Thus, to be concerned about Faye's latest release "Sing & Play", no matter in the promotions or the production side, all have a very high standard, beilev "A-Mei" still need to work harder.

..........................

You buy "Faye Wong", have u ask yourself why ? Maybe you dun believe Faye herself and the record company, or manager company can be so good, but the above analysis can really be explained for the above. The fact is " Faye Wong" success can be said is not a purposely made result. Want to surpass the past whole list , is not only lower down two keys can made it. Faye from the beginning to now never under the table to fight for two more awards or two more movies. It's also because of this type of let things happen naturally that makes her became Big today , really makes the writer very interested, so he wants to try to put this case to analysis from a 'lazy' way of thinking.

In order to research on "Faye Wong", flip through a whole stack of magazines, actually Faye in 1990 already say want to learn Japanese to enter into the International scene, in 1991say want to be like idol, Teresa Teng to be famous in Asia, Also, in 1992, she already know she will get married and have a child.Result is Faye no need to learn Japanese also can be famous in a foreign land, "Fei Mi Mi Zhi Yin" is a tribute to her idol , she is more fortunate than Teng, DouWei and TongTong is at her side each.

From Wang Jing Wen sing till Faye Wong, has already been 10 years, in this 10 years for Faye can be said to have found a journey to find and make her dreams come true.

Hong Kong music scene needs Faye Wong, because she definitely clearly knows what she is doing. No matter is right or wrong, is also her own choice.
