Chapter 7

Sales Analysis and Reporting System

Enabling rich, web-based data analysis and reporting systems was one of our team's key goals when we designed the Microsoft Office Web Components. In this chapter, I will describe my implementation of such a system and show you how to combine a number of the techniques presented in Part I to build a sales analysis and reporting system for your company. Although the solution in this chapter focuses on sales information, the techniques used here are applicable to any type of data.
