Conclusion

In this chapter, you've seen both list view and tree view demonstration applications. In addition, you've seen the two combined into a reasonably powerful, "real world" application, FileMgr.

In fact, by now you've seen demonstration applications of many of the widgets that make up WFC. However, for some applications these premade widgets just won't do. In those cases, you need the capability to draw to the display yourself. The next chapter demonstrates how to draw on the display freehand.
