Extraordinary 3,000 seconds

(HK Exclusive Interview) Extraordinary 3,000 seconds - Faye Wong.
From i-weekly , Text : Liu Jie Qi 

Before flying off to Hong Kong to have my interview with Faye, the record company promotions manager told me :" this is faye since she entered into the music scene to date, first ever formally accept a singapore face to face media interview, it is really considered a very rare chance. She jokingly said : "can be considered is like ' the big empress' specially give face, rite ?"

But then, not till the last second, no one can guarantee this interview with faye can go smoothly, if didn't see her real person, there's no confirmed outcome.

Maybe is because of this reason, or maybe listen to too many reports, everyone's hearts is feeling worried, especially is the reporter from "8 days" that is going on this trip too, who was very interesting. Before starting off to the interview, was quickly browsing through all her information, taking deep breathes, in the end almost resulted in her gastric pain to happen.

I consoled her :" you relax a bit okie, faye is not any three heads or six shoulders creature, she is also just a person." Just like this, we got onto the record company's car and reached the Jiu Long Zhuo Dun Dao " Ye Kuang Shang Ye Da Sha."

When the big door is pushed open, in the small meeting room, faye who was chatting with the make-up artist, zing turn around and look at us, was smiling and said hello to us. A simple light colour T-shirt, a pair of blue jeans, a calm face. Interview starts, interview ends. All we saw, is but a person.

Faye Wong Live Interview Report Date : 22nd September 1998 (Tuesday) Time : Evening 6.08 pm to 6.58pm Place : Hong Kong's Jiu Long Zhuo Dun Dao " Ye Kuang Shang Ye Da Sha" Person : Faye Wong , i-weekly Liu Jie Qi, 8-days Andy Chen, also Zing, record company person-in-charge ah han, fans -- our mag photography reporter Alfie Lee & special image director Daniel Goh.

(Below dialogue, "Fei" represents Faye Wong, " Jie" represents Liu Jie Qi)

Jie : In your this new album, the style seems to go into many different types of direction, what is the most important thing that you wish to express to music listeners ? Fei : Express what thing ah ? also not expressing much lah , actually ..how to say ah , actually every time before do one album, I also never think of what to express a particular issue or what, only wish to make this album to be very good, if I can be totally involved into the compositions, that is the best lah ! However, mostly I dun make many compositions, but will try my best to put in more of my own things inside, just like that. Actually express something or not is open to the audience's opinions, I only wish to do a good album.

Jie : In your albums, there is more and more of your own compositions, in the near future will there be a full album of all of your own things ? Fei : Really hope can be like this, but I feel that currently I still cannot fully do that. Because although now, I only release one album per year, but I still feel that the time is not short.

Jie : Then what u feel is the most ideal time to complete one album ? Fei : This is very hard to say, cannot guarantee is 3 or 5 years , 8 years or ....keke (Faye's laughter : is between those ha and he, not those haha ...big laugh type, also not those from the sound that comes from the nose, so is "kekeke" this type.) Actually my ideal thought is to make my album as my interest and not a job, maybe I can do that.

Jie : Many singers hope to at least release 2 albums and above per year to let their popularity to be stable and to get a stable income. As for your style of only releasing one album per year, as compared with the normal singers who follow the pattern, is there any bad points to that ? Is there any problems to maintain ? Fei : I dun feel is hard to maintain, living can still maintain ah ! keke ....but if spend a very long time to produce an album that yourself likes a lot, I feel is really worth it. Maybe you one year release 2 albums, but if is of a poor quality, the effect that comes out is not good ....I feel the most important is still quality.

(At this time, Andy asked her about the conflict between interest and work , she replied :" now is already very hard to differentiate between interest and work, so sometimes will be in a dilemma, because music , itself is not a business product, but now is already as a business product that is popular, thus very naturally will result in this very hard to solve dilemma. "Unless I dun continue to sign on with any record company, only purely doing my own music, but can also stably maintain my life, that I think will be my most happiest time." As she says this, she smiles again.)

Jie : Will u feel you are under no choice when faced with those questions regarding the selling amounts of your albums and the market problems, like you are living in a very hard to control surroundings ? Fei : Yes, thus need to make a balance, but I feel it should not be like that. Like the environment that I am now in, I feel should go and face it, and not just feel no choice, thus I feel have to make a balance. To do it is quite hard, but I will try my best to do it.

(Andy wish to understand why the song she wrote for her daughter "Tong" goes a dancing style kind, she replied : " many songs that are written for kids are those heart-warming and soft types, actually heart-warming things need not be only express in soft ways, because she is very active. This song is douwei who arranges it together, before that we got discussed to co-operate to make a dance kind of song, thus when this is finished , we feel is very suitable for Tong Tong."

Jie : This song, have u let Tong Tong listen ? Did she dance with the music ? Fei : Yes, yes !

Jie : She likes this song ? Fei : Yup, I think she likes it. (smiles)

Jie : In the song, the instruments that are used brings out a bit of Indian taste, is this what u want ? Fei : Got meh ? This question u have to ask douwei, music is he who arranges it, personally I dun feel the song have any Indian taste.

Jie : The image in this album seems to give people the feeling of going back to the innocent look type, just like what the case wrote :" Faye never before pure innocent romantic, just like under the sun, because of playfulness got a sunburnt on the face like a kid", is this your most innocent and best reflection ? Fei : ( immediately turned to sitting next to her, Zing) you explain lah ........case sometimes is not really .....actually I never really think carefully of what image to use, also never really seriously go and plan, I all give to Zing , art director, hairstylist and photographer, they will have meetings and discuss about it , actually is all very natural, goes with the feelings .......I think the main thing they want is still to represent me ? ....keke ....represents character, look more natural !

" Actually I'm not like what the outside people discussed me as so cold, maybe is because due to my character, I'm not good at socializing, will not take the initiative, but if become friends, I'm quite active one, and can be very wild ....."

Jie : That is also the more true you ? Fei : There's no really true or false I guess ? No matter what, he do what ever make-up or drew anything on my face, still need me to express it out, thus no need to say true or false, all is me.

Jie : Basically you will not have any objections ? Fei : Sometimes , some images I cannot accept or dun like ...but usually will not have, because we have been working together for very long, are friends, they all understand me, all have a kind of chemistry, kno what suits me, what I dun like.

Jie : You give people the impression of " I do my own business" kind of image, towards music is also the same manner, basically do u concern about the direction of the music industry ? concern about the whole of the music world ? Fei : Is not that I am not concern, my music still starts from myself, I will not do what is popular in the market now, I dun necessarily follow it, main thing still lies from my own likes.

Jie : Your good friend, Naying in a recent telephone interview told the audience : " privately faye is very friendly to guests, love friends, love fun, sometimes can get wild , she is like a totally different person from what you all see her". To what she said, you have any agree or disagree points? Fei : I dun disagree, actually me ...how to say leh ? Actually I'm not like what the outside people say me as that cold, maybe is to strangers. Maybe is because of my character, I'm not too good at socializing, will not take the initiative, but if become friends, I'm quite active one, and can be very wild, keke....but no choice lah, audience still is audience, no matter how he likes my songs, there's still a distance between us, it is not possible that he can fully understand me. I feel there is also no choice here, I also dun wish to force them to fully understand me, as long they listen to my songs, like my songs, that is good enough.

( Based on character questions, Andy asked Faye what matters will make her feel very happy? Faye think for a while and said :" many things can make me very happy and not happy, a small matter can make me very happy, but can also make me unhappy, I feel I am a sensitive person, emotions tend to change quite greatly. Actually I'm not much different from the past, still in the state of dilemma .....human basically is a dilemma ! " When she was saying this, she was smiling , but there seems to have some dilemma expression in that smile.)

Jie : After having Tong Tong, your attitude towards life , is there any changes ? Fei : Actually many thing have two sides, being a mother of course is very happy, but also have many worries, many troubles....being a person is like that, good and bad, right and wrong both sides also got.

Jie : What about the way u view things ? Any changes ? Fei : I'm always quite a optimistic person, even if my emotions is in a bad times, I will still try to find a good direction to think, past is like that, now is also like that.

Jie : There's people who view life as very very important, will you in the limited life span, very enthusiastic wish to do all that you wish to do ? Fei : I cannot say be very enthusiastic, also not enthusiastic, I will try my best, main thing is let nature take its own course lor ! In this way is more comfortable.

Jie : You will not force what ? Fei : Right ah, many things you cannot force one.

Jie : What is your ideal life ? Fei : Ideal life ? Ideal life ....( stoned for almost 18secs, can sense that she is seriously thinking about this question) actually I feel that there is no really an ideal life, what I mean is " if now you think the future you will be " like this like this" thats good enough, but when reached "like this like this " later, you might still not be satisfied, will always wish for more things. Thus I will not go and think next time what I wish to be like, I only wish to face the problems in the current times, I feel this is life, dun think too far.

(At this point, Andy asked her what she feels for Kit Chan performance in the " Slowly walk life". Faye's first expression is laughing greatly:"she finished acting already meh ? haven't yet right !" But after a short while, she realizes she seems to be a bit overdoing it and think have misunderstood if the reply to the question is not good, thus she immediately calm down and say something:" Kit Chan not bad, I always feel she is someone who can sing, she is quite suitable in the music world too, as compared to me, I'm always scared of acting." These few words dissolve the almost reach awkward situation.)

Jie : Because you are scared of acting, thus after "Chungking Express", there's no more new works ? Fei : At this point no yet, but I will not say I throw away acting in movies totally, but I must defintely have the interest, and I must have confidence in myself.

Jie : Wong Kar Wai will not let u off easily / Fei : (smiles) He got find me before, we are still in the discussing part, still have not reach the final decision.

Jie : This album called " Songs of Wanderlust", is the production crew's idea or yours ? Fei : Is the production crew. Actually my thought is to call it "Faye Wong", because I have no concept, I only wish to do a better listening album, I dun have any particular thing to express out, not much concept. I suggested named "Faye Wong" is good, but they say is the same as the last album ......

"You all feel Faye Wong do not know how to smile, right ? Really that's too horrible ! haha......"

Jie : We saw the album cover and inside pictures of this latest album, all very very surprised :actually faye can be so cute, can be so fun ......... Fei : Haha ......you all feel Faye Wong will not smile, right ? Really that is too horrible! haha.....

Jie : In 1994 "Movies double issue ", there's an article interview and you said the job that you dun wish to take is do "interviews", wonder after so many years, have your attitude towards media change ? Will you be soften down a bit ? Fei : The situation now does not allows me to soften down, I feel that the HongKong media is overdoing it now, becomes more serious. Actually my attitude changes in the past is not because after I became famous, then change to not being co-operative. Is because I feel that some Hong Kong media are too unreasonable, I dun wish to have anything to do with them. If in doing work, I will not mind , what makes me feel dislike them is their too much of their following you and chasing. Like today we here doing the interview, I did not have any objections. Because if they did something that makes me feel uncomfortable, I will use one attitude towards them. I always dun like people to interfere with my personal matters, I feel everyone always dun wish that. Some artists will feel is part of their job, cannot avoid, but I will not allow such thing to happen, if they have ways to secretly take photos, whatever, I have no way to stop them, but its not possible for me to work with them on this matter, attitude will not soften down, I'm forever like this.

There's something about Faye Fei Ren Fei Shi Very funny, this time to Hong Kong to interview Faye, because totally dun have any confidence, thus beforehand prepare a lot of "incase" type of job, if incase cannot have an interview, still have something to bring back. Thus, I did a "songs link questionair", and is through many asking and investigations, from faye loyal fans gathered all those they wish to know and still dun know " Fei Ren Fei Shi " questionairs. In the end, not only the interview ended successfully, still have lots of time to do this "fans questionair", please read !

Qn : Represents fans's questions Ans : Represents faye's answers

Qn : Which is your most fav song of Teresa Teng ? Ans : Too hard to say, almost every one of her songs I like.

Qn : Can play any instrument ? Ans : No

Qn : What is the most touching event that a fan has done for you ? Ans : ( stopped for 16secs) I cannot remember.

Qn : Besides singing, what else do u very "crazily" like ? Ans : I love to play, as long is play, I like. ( ask Zing next to her) I play mahjong can be considered wild ?

Qn : What is your most fav singapore food ? Ans : That that green cake .....( Zing smilingly said : "is pandan cake.") Because the impression is the deepest, other than that I have not eaten much. Oh , still have hainanese chicken rice, I feel is quite nice, better than Hong Kong.

Qn : If the terms allows, what is the thing that u wish to change most in life ? Ans : Never think before.

Qn : What is the degree of your spects ? Ans : 400 degrees, have a bit of 'shan guang" too but its mild.

Qn : Normally need to leave her daughter for how long, then u will miss her ? Ans : Cannot say lah ! Depends on what I am doing, if I'm playing mahjong .....( everyone understands her meaning as : if I'm playing mahjong, can forget for a bit longer! Thus, everyone laughs, she also keke...smiles.)

Qn : Now what you feel is different about the media in different areas ( Hong Kong, Taiwan, Singapore, Japan, China ) ? Ans : Actually Beijing media seems like I haven't have contact before, I only live there, very rarely have interviews .....actually the media that I have most contact is the Hong Kong media, I think the most uncomfortable is the Hong Kong media, because they are nearer to me, I spend a lot of time in Hong Kong, Thus they have many chances to follow me, maybe if I live in Taiwan will also have such things ?

Qn : Where do u wish to spend most of your time to live ? Ans : France, Paris. Because I like. Paris gives me the feeling of living in Beijing, feel very free.

Qn : For one fan that has not been to Beijing, what is the most attractive thing about Beijing? Ans: Beijing is a historical place, have many historical monuments, places of interests ...of course, I live in Beijing not because of that, keke.......that is family, family feeling ....this should be easily understood rite ? At home is where I feel the most freedom.

Faye's autograph to i-weekly readers (in chinese): Singapore friends : Wish happy! signed by faye

Me & Faye Two feet away story

When the interview was over, Faye open her eyes big big and asked :" finished liao ?" cannot believe we so easily "let her go".

But I still hear her softly gave a sign of relief, smiling stood up and twist her waist, continue to chat with her other company people happily, the atmposhere was very easy and happy.

Actually, to everyone present who wasn't give a sign of relief ?

When we came out of the building, my companion, Daniel very excitedly said :" Faye is simply too good, I love her even more !"

Everyone was very happy, feel that this interview was too perfect, no any hurting event.

After returned to Singapore, with colleagues, friends and family, whenever I talk about this interview, all have the same two questions : " You really face to face talk with her for 50 mins ? what is she like ?" Looks like , Faye leaves people the deep impression of her like " cannot be near, better not to trifle with, very hard to handle". However, if you see the above live interview , you should be able to make a decision on her character now.

The interview of me and faye, was in a small meeting room, on a circular desk, her every moment , every smile was clearly shown.

Frankly speaking, Faye is just like anyone of us. When she talks, it is full of expressions, black and big eyes always love to turn here and there, looks like a naughty child. She also have her own friends, love and family. Talk about them, when mention about some funny events, she will also laugh hehe ..haha .....

If think carefully, I feel that Faye is someone who cannot do business, Hong Kong, Taiwan and China any one is a bigger market than Singapore rite or not ? Why she rather choose our interview , and not spend more time wih the other three medias ?

There's only one sentence to say : Faye only wish to do things accordingly to her own likes, she wants to be true to herself, because she knows, people still need to face yourself in the end. Thus she said :" I am like that in the past, now is like that, forever is like that."
