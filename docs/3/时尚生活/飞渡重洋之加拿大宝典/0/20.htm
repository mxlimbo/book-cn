加国移民申请材料

　　Documents required of all applicants:所有申请者要求提供文件

　　Household Register and Labour Insurance Cards along with certified English or French translation. 身份证或工作证,带英文或法文翻译的

　　Your marriage certificate.：婚姻证明

　　your spouse's birth certificate.：配偶出生证明

　　Your children's birth certificate.：子女出生证明

　　Proof of your children's continuous enrollment in full-time studies since reaching 19 years of age(or if married before reaching 19, since the marriage).：19岁或以上子女或小于19岁但已婚子女的全时入读证明

　　Adoption/divorce and child custody documents.：养或离婚子女的监护文件

　　Independant Skilled Worker or Assisted Relative applicants: 独立技术或受助关系申请者提供文件

　　Diplomas, school certificates or apprenticeship cetificates.：学位证书或学徒证明

　　Detailed letters of reference from employer stating job duties：工作经历推荐信

　　Proof of relationship to any parent, brother, sister, uncle, aunt, niece or nephew residing in Canada.：在加拿大长期居住的亲属关系证明:父母,兄第，姐妹，叔婶，侄子　

　　Evidence of your relative's permanent resident status or Canadian citizenship.：你亲属的永久居民身份或加拿大公民身份证明。

　　Family Class applicants: 家庭团聚申请者提供文件 :

　　Relationship proof with your sponsor in Canada.：与加拿大资助人的关系证明

　　Evidence of sponsor's permanent resident status or Canadian citizenship.：资助人的永久居民身份或加拿大公民身份证明

　　Spouse questionnaire.：配偶问卷表

　　Fiance(e) questionnaire and declaration of intention to marry. ：准备结婚的资产调查和申报

　　Relationship proof with your spouse/fiance(e) in Canada, e.g. photos of you and your spouse/fiance(e)together, letters with envelopes and postmarks, long distance phone bill, etc.：与加拿大配偶或未婚夫/妻的关系证明:照片，信件，长途电话单等

　　Fully completed Business Applicant Summary form.：完整填写的商业申请表

　　Fully completed Personal Net Worth form.：个人净资产表

　　Bank letters stating any outstanding mortgages or other liabilities.：关于未偿付的借贷或负债的怠行信件

　　Government documents supporting the applicant for business registration. ：政府批复的企业注册文件

　　Profit/loss statements, balance sheet, profit tax assessments,profits tax computations and personal tax assessments for most recent three years. ：近三年的损益表和税表
