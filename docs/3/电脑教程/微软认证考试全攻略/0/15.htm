Additional components added in to IEAK that are custom, must be digitally signed and in What types
of format?
A. (. dat)
B. (. ico)
C. (. exe)
D. (. cab)

be :
A. Approved by version
B. Digitally signed
C. Approved by IE development team
D. Compressed for installation
