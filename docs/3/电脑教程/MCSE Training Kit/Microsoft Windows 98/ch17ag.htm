Practice: Configuring Microsoft NetMeeting

In this practice, you will configure Microsoft NetMeeting.

    To configure Microsoft NetMeeting

    1. Click Start, point to Programs, Internet Explorer, and then click Microsoft NetMeeting.

      The Microsoft NetMeeting Wizard appears.

    2. Click Next.

      You are prompted to log on to a directory server.

    3. Type in the name of your organization's directory server and then click Next.

      You are prompted for personal information.

    4. Type in your first name, last name, and e-mail address, and then click Next.

    5. Click For business use and then click Next.

      You are prompted for your connection speed.

    6. Click your connection speed and then click Next.

    7. Select your network connection and then click Next

      The Audio Tuning Wizard appears.

    8. Click Next.

    9. Click Test.

    10. Adjust the volume to a comfortable level and then click Next.

    11. Adjust the recording volume, using the quoted text, and then click Next

    12. Click Finish.

Note

For more information, refer to http://www.microsoft.com/netmeeting.
