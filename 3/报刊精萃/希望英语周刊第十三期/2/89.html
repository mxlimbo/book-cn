孩 子 们 的 好 伙 伴
 　
　 This classroom is always full of fun.It seems this young foreign teacher has made it into play room.Students are so enchanted by the class that they have become unaware of the cameras in the room. In our Wanquan Primary School,we call our foreign teacher Lang Da. She dances well and is good at singing. In class,Lang Da surely has a way of gaining attention,so we can easily learn the new things from her. Lang Da has the heart of a child.She loves to stay with the students, and sometimes she takes us to her dormitory,where we tell stories and sing songs together.We get along with her very well. In my family,there was always music.Both my parents are musical,so I grow up loving music,because my father was always singing and my mother always had classical music on. So I grow up loving music.That's a part of the reason I want to become a music teacher.So I grow up loving children, and also loving music.So that's my two loves. Lang Da told us,that she grew up in a large family.She has three sis- ters and one little brother,so Lang Da has loved meeting people since she was a little girl.Her major in college was Music Education,that's why she sings so well. In the United States,I was trained to be a music teacher,so I have taught a little bit of music,some private lessons,voice and piano,and also I worked at a kids club for quite a few years.I worked with kids once a week,teaching them doing games and telling them stories. That experience.I really enjoy that.That's why I got back into teaching for a time because I really enjoy working with kids. Lang Da once worked in an international department of a large computer company.As a result,she had many opportunities to meet people from around the world. Gradually she started developing an interest in Chinese culture. That's what I realize how differently different cultures think.Because they are… their way of doing business and their whole way of thinking and looking at problems or solving things was so diffrent from the way I thought. There is a whole diffeerent culture and a whole different way of life and way of things.So I decided that it would be interesting to come to China and live here and find out what kinds of things impacted people or made them think so differently from the way I learned. Lang Da has been in China for more than three years.Now she is teaching English to First Grade students.Lang Da says,Chinese students pay more attention to discipline,and are more capable at memorizing and training.But in America,students are more active,and they like to express themselves freely.These are very different teaching environments for children. I like the students to be able to think about what I am saying.Like,some of the things I will do is if I ask them something in English… if I had taught them something in English,like,everymorning I say“ Good morning stu- dents,please sit down.” I want them to think about what that means,not“ oh, now it is time for me to sit down.” So in the morning when I say“ good mor- ning” ,sometimes I will change it and say“ please touch your head” and if they are not thinking,then they will sit down and then all the other students will laugh,because they know they did not listen to what I have said. Lang Da mentioned,she loves teaching at our school,because her teaching can help us grow,and learn how to get along with other people.Lang Da said, it's interesting to watch the children study.She feels happy to see their progress. Lang Da has won our hearts with her teaching and her kindness.I guess here in Beijing,she has found a worthwhile place for herself. We sincerely wish her all of the happiness that life in China has to offer.
