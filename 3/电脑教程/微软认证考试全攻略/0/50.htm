70-059

Just passed the TCP/IP exam today, Jan 8,1999. The first 15-20 questions

wre all Trancenders happy. ( I even got asked 4 indentical questions with

all the same answers). Adaptive is GOOD!!!! Don't get intimidated by it.

1. The backbone of your network is a WNT serv. Lately it's been

experiecing some router overloading or saturation. What do you use to check

it?

A. ICMP source quench (received)

2. Drag and Drop- DHCP definitions:

Scope option, DHCP relay agent (answer is remote subnet way of obtaining IP

addr.),Global, exclusion, client option.

3. IP config window with an IP address and subnet mask. What are 2 valid

host I.D.

A. remember that they both have to be in the same subnet. Ex. a mask of

255.255.252.0- intervals of 8. Thus 0-7, 8-15..etc.

4. Two questions on wkst.1 could not connect to a local host and remote

host (same wordings)

A. invalid subnet mask A. Invalid subnet mask. (no option for D.G.)

5. You want tosee port status?

A. nbtstat or netstat? ( i think it's nbtstat)

6. Two scenario on Unix and WINNT to be able to browse each other on the

network...

A. Know your DNS-Hostfile, WINS-LMhostfile, static entries....etc..

7. Sunet 1 and Subnet 2 exibit (trancenders) wrkst.1 could not connect to

Server on local destination.

A. The exibit shows invalid D.G. (router interface) for 2 wksts. to throw

you off. But you know better. WRONG D.G.

8. You can UNC a server on remote but could not FTP.

A. no access to FTP directory (braindump)

9. Third DNS on the net to use for the internet...

A. (no option to configure with cache).....only use as a forwarder for the

prim. and sec.

10. Subnets with WINS and UNIX machines....

A. know where to put your dhcp relay agents, UNIX does not use WINS

LMHOST...(Trancenders happy-many questions like these).

Good luck and contribute to braindump......3 more to go!!!!!!!!!
