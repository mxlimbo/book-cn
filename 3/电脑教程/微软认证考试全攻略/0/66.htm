70-064Windows 95试题回顾（4）

Windows 95

Q: You have a windows 95 computer with a hardrive that keeps getting out of

memory messages and high disk activity (paging)...You had another hardrive

and have more room for your files but you are still having high disk

activity on the first hardrive... What can you do with the swap file? Four

choices... I chose to move it to the new drive..the other choices concerned

reconfiguring the size of the swap file.

Q: You upgrade your computer that has 2 hardrives from win 3.1 to 95...One

of the hardrives is not functioning well...What can you do? I said "Disable

32 bit hard disk drivers".

Q: A person complains that his application (new app that was just added) is

running slow. You choose to use system monitor to monitor the application?

What do you choose to monitor? Kernel;Processor %.

Q: Your Computer has user level security...Carol has access to the

\\server1\Public folder, but, does not have access to the

\\server1\Public\Customer folder. How can you give her access to the

customer folder? The answer I believe is by providing explicit permissions

to the customer folder. The other choices that i can remember were...Provide

implicit permissions to the customer folder..Provide Full access to the

public folder( this option was not correct because you only want to give her

access to one subfolder (customer) not all of the subfolders.

Q: You have a computer on a lan with an Ethernet adapter installed with

TCP/IP and you connect to the internet using a modem cable. What would you

do to disable people from gaining access to your files on your LAN?

A. Disable File and Print sharing for the Ethernet adapter

B. Disable file and print sharing for the modem cable.

Q: You add a plug and play printer to your 95 computer. How would you

install the drivers?

A. Install bi-directional 1284ee compliant cable

B. Install a standard LPT1 port

C. Install a bi-directional port

D. ? can't remember

Had to choose two of the above: I found this question to be very strange.

Q: You had some type of SCSI hardware to your computer. Where in the

registry would you find information on it?

A. Hkey_User

B. HKey_Current_User

C. Hkey_Local_Machine

D. Hkey_root

I picked c.
