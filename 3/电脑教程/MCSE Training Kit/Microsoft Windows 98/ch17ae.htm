Lesson 4: Communicating on the Internet and Intranets

This lesson provides an overview of two tools in Windows 98 that enhance your ability to communicate across intranets and the Internet. With NetMeeting, you can hold audio and video conferences with remote sites. With Outlook Express, you can send and receive Internet e-mail that supports secure Multipurpose Internet Mail Extensions (s/MIME), which enables messages to be encrypted and digitally signed.

After this lesson, you will be able to:

  • Configure NetMeeting.

  • Configure Outlook Express.

Estimated lesson time: 25 minutes
