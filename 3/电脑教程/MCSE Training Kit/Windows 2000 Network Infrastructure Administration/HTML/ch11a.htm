Chapter 11

Providing Your Clients Remote Access Service (RAS)

About This Chapter

In this chapter, you will learn how to implement remote access services to provide your clients the ability to access network resources from the road or their homes. You will also learn how to implement secure virtual private networks (VPNs).

Before You Begin

To complete this chapter, you must have

  • Two Microsoft Windows 2000 servers with local area network (LAN) connectivity
